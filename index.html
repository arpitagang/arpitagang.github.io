Arpita Gang

I am a PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering department at Rutgers University working under the supervision of Prof. Waheed Bajwa. My research interests lie in the areas of distributed optimization, machine learning and information processing.

Prior to this, I graduated with a Master's degree from Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology, Delhi (IIITD) in 2016. I was also a Research Assistant at IIITD from 2016-17 where I worked on audio signal processing, specifically on source separation problem.
